# Vetted Video Channels

The pre-vetted channel database for classroom media. Reliable channels — large subscriber bases, consistent upload history, rarely delete content, generally ad-light within educational content — grouped by area. This is a growing data reference: the `media-processing` handover loads it when building a candidate link set, and adds new channels here as they prove out. Confirm currency at build time; treat the list as a starting point, not a closed set.
